Factions

Explore the various factions fighting in Helldivers 2.

Super Earth

The beacon of freedom and democracy in the galaxy. Home to Helldivers and the only power standing against tyranny, infestation, and religious fanaticism.

The Terminids

Genetically modified bugs running amok. These fascists are overwhelming and relentless.

The Automatons

A machine uprising from Cyberstan turned into militant socialist dystopia, the Automatons seek to dismantle human supremacy.

The Illuminate

A returned empire of energy-wielding fanatics, employing energy shields, jetpacks, and the worst weapon of all: voter suppression!
